Stuff About Me

Games

I am an avid gamer who really likes video games and tabletop games. I also like card games, particularly CCGs, though most of my favorite CCGs have sadly ended. In terms of video games, I tend to be more of a fan of older games, mostly platform games and jRPGs. Wild ARMS, Persona, Sonic and Mega Man are some favorite franchises of mine.

Stories

I am also a writer and reader. So much so that my BA from UCSC is in English Literature, though I prefer the creative side of writing much more than the technical one. While I read a variety of things, I prefer to write fantasy and science fiction. I like larger than life characters and events, and it's harder to do that and remain plausible in more contemporary real world fiction. Inspirations of mine include Star Trek and many other television shows from the 80s and 90s era; many comics, cartoons and anime; as well as authors like Madeleine L'Engle and J.K. Rowling, among others. I also write fanfiction on occasion, mostly of super hero comics, with X-Men being a favorite of mine.
